# Dashboard Walkthrough

## Purpose

This dashboard enables stakeholders to evaluate experiment performance, understand conversion behavior, and identify opportunities for optimization.

---

## Section 1: Experiment Overview

Displays:

* Experiment Name
* Experiment Dates
* Traffic Allocation
* Statistical Confidence Level

Purpose:

Provides context for interpreting all reported metrics.

---

## Section 2: KPI Summary

Displays:

* Sessions
* CTA Click Rate
* Conversion Rate
* Relative Lift

Purpose:

Provides a high-level performance snapshot.

Recommended Action:

Review before analyzing detailed breakdowns.

---

## Section 3: Conversion Funnel

Displays:

Visitors
↓
CTA Clicks
↓
Form Starts
↓
Lead Submissions

Purpose:

Identify where users exit the conversion journey.

Recommended Action:

Investigate stages with unusually high drop-off rates.

---

## Section 4: Variant Comparison

Displays side-by-side performance for:

* Control
* Variant B

Metrics:

* Conversion Rate
* Click Through Rate
* Lift

Purpose:

Evaluate experiment effectiveness.

---

## Section 5: Segment Analysis

Displays:

* Device Performance
* Traffic Source Performance
* Geographic Performance

Purpose:

Identify audience segments generating the highest incremental impact.

Recommended Action:

Use findings to prioritize future optimization opportunities.

---

## Section 6: Statistical Validation

Displays:

* P-Value
* Confidence Interval
* Statistical Significance Indicator

Purpose:

Confirm whether observed differences are likely due to the experiment rather than random variation.

---

## Section 7: Business Impact

Displays:

* Incremental Leads
* Pipeline Impact
* Revenue Estimates

Purpose:

Translate experiment results into business outcomes.

Recommended Action:

Use impact estimates to support rollout decisions.
