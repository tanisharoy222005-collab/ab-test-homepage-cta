# Metric Calculations

## Sessions

Definition:

Total number of experiment sessions included in the analysis.

Formula:

Sessions = Count(Distinct Session ID)

---

## CTA Click Rate

Definition:

Percentage of users who clicked the CTA after viewing it.

Formula:

CTA Click Rate = CTA Clicks / CTA Impressions

Example:

1,775 Clicks / 25,000 Impressions

= 7.1%

---

## Form Start Rate

Definition:

Percentage of users who initiated the lead form.

Formula:

Form Starts / Sessions

---

## Lead Conversion Rate

Definition:

Percentage of users who completed the lead form successfully.

Formula:

Conversions / Sessions

Example:

1,150 Conversions / 25,000 Sessions

= 4.6%

---

## Relative Lift

Definition:

Percentage improvement of Variant B compared to Control.

Formula:

((Variant CR - Control CR) / Control CR) × 100

Example:

((4.6 - 4.1) / 4.1) × 100

= 12.2%

---

## Revenue Per Visitor

Definition:

Average revenue generated per experiment session.

Formula:

Revenue / Sessions

---

## Incremental Leads

Definition:

Additional leads generated due to the winning variant.

Formula:

(Variant Conversion Rate - Control Conversion Rate)
× Total Sessions

---

## Pipeline Impact

Definition:

Estimated pipeline value attributable to experiment improvements.

Formula:

Incremental Leads
× Lead-to-Opportunity Rate
× Average Deal Value

---

## Statistical Significance

Definition:

Likelihood that observed differences occurred due to the experiment rather than random chance.

Threshold:

P-Value < 0.05

Confidence Level:

95%
