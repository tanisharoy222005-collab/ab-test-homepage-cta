# Monitoring Plan

## Daily Checks

- Event volume consistency
- Traffic allocation validation
- Conversion tracking accuracy

## Weekly Checks

- Lead volume trends
- Device-level anomalies
- Traffic source shifts

## Escalation Triggers

### Event Drop

Threshold:

20% decrease day-over-day

### Conversion Drop

Threshold:

15% decrease day-over-day

### Traffic Imbalance

Threshold:

Variant allocation exceeds 55/45 split
