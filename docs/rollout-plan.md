---

## Rollback Plan

Rollback conditions include:

### Tracking Failure

Critical analytics events stop firing or data collection becomes unreliable.

### Lead Quality Decline

Qualified lead rate decreases by more than 10%.

### Revenue Impact

Revenue per visitor decreases by more than 5%.

### Technical Issues

User experience degradation or page performance issues are identified.

If any rollback condition is met, traffic will immediately revert to the Control experience while investigation occurs.
